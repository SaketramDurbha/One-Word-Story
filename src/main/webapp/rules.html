rules

Please follow these rules.

◆ Try to make the story make sense and follow the moods and chapter names! There's no fun if you just type random words.

◆ Your word cannot be one of the last three submitted words.

◆ Your word must be under 10 characters

◆ Your word must be in the dictionary

◆ Your word can't be explicit

◆ Your word must be lower-case (the program will take care of capitalization)

◆ Your word must be only using letters. Numbers and other symbols cannot be in your words.
