what is this place?

This is One Word Lifetime.

What is basically happening is a massive one-word story. Each user contributes words anonymously and it's added to the ongoing story. The story, after 50 chapters, becomes a book that anyone can read any time.

How do I submit?

It's pretty easy. Just type a word in the box at the bottom and hit enter or “add!” Be sure to follow the rules. Your word will be shown to the world and cannot be deleted, so be sure that the word you've submitted is what you actually want to submit.

How is punctuation placed?

Periods are placed after a random amount of words. Paragraphs are placed after a random amount of periods, and chapters are placed after a random amount of paragraphs! Books are always completed after 50 chapters, though. You can see how many of something is left by pressing the more button on the header.
